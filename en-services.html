SERVICES

  • Physiotherapy
    Physiotherapy

    Try physiotherapy if you want to actively contribute to improve your movement abilities, to reduce pain, help speedy recovery, delay or even avoid surgical treatment, and prevent injuries.

    Make an appointment if you have problems such as...

    • Painful musculoskeletal conditions, such as back pain, joint pain, headaches
    • Muscle aches, insertion pain of tendons and ligaments such as Achilles tendon inflammation or tennis elbow
    • Difficulties associated with functional disorders of the musculoskeletal system (joint blockage, muscle spasms, problems with scars)
    • Intervertebral disc herniation, tingling in the extremities
    • Postoperative conditions
    • Parkinson's disease, facial palsy, post-stroke conditions, multiple sclerosis
    • Functional sterility
    • Rheumatological disease (ankylosing spondylitis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis joints)
  • Sports Physiotherapy
    Sports Physiotherapy

    Sports physiotherapy is open to all athletes who want to improve their performance at the professional level. Sportspersons who want to prevent injuries and overloading, and regenerate properly. I can help with sports injuries and a quick return to good shape!

    • Stretching education
    • Efficiency of sport-specific movement (hitting a ball)
    • Strengthening
    • Correct sitting on the bike
    • Fitness training
    • Sports equipment (Gymball, Overball, Bosu, Flowin, Flexibar, Multisport trainer (TRX) …)
