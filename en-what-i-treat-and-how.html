WHAT I TREAT AND HOW

  • I can help you
    Make an appointment if you have problems such as...
    • Painful musculoskeletal conditions, such as back pain, joint pain, headaches
    • Muscle aches, insertion pain of tendons and ligaments such as Achilles tendon inflammation or tennis elbow
    • Difficulties associated with functional disorders of the musculoskeletal system (joint blockage, muscle spasms, problems with scars)
    • Intervertebral disc herniation, tingling in the extremities
    • Postoperative conditions
    • Parkinson's disease, facial palsy, post-stroke conditions, multiple sclerosis
    • Functional sterility
    • Rheumatological disease (ankylosing spondylitis, rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis joints)
  • For who?
    For who?
    • Everyone who wants to participate in their own well-being
    • Patients with orthopaedic, neurological diseases and Rheumatology
    • Athletes of all levels and abilities
    • Children
    • People working in offices, manual workers, people with stressful jobs
  • Therapeutic approaches
    Therapeutic approaches

    First step:
    Entry examination: Initial Assessment - kinesiology analysis, goal setting, draft of a rehabilitation plan
    Further steps:
    A procedure according to a kinesiology analysis from the beginning, elimination of the causes of the problem. Assessment of the current situation, new exercises, correction exercises and eventually learning their progress.

    The number of intervals and visits are part of the plan and are dependent on the development of the client’s status.

    • Kinesiology analysis
    • Soft tissue techniques
    • Mobilization of the spine and peripheral joints
    • Exercise on the principles of developmental kinesiology
    • Sensomotoric stimulation
    • Kinesio taping
    • SM system
    • Flowin
    • Nordic walking
    • Vojta reflex locomotion
    • Proprioceptive neuromuscular stimulation
    • Pelvic floor exercises to aid female sterility and incontinence
    • Exercising with a Gymball and Overballs
    • Physiotherapy equipment (Bosu, Flexibar, Multisport trainer (TRX) ...)

  • The use of exercise equipment
    The use of exercise equipment

    Use equipment for activation and compensation musculoskeletal system (Gymball, Overballs, Bosu, Flowin, Flexibar, TRX,...)
